Relationship Between HCF and LCM:

The Highest Common Factor (HCF) and Least Common Multiple (LCM) are two important concepts in mathematics used to analyze and compare numbers. There is a key relationship between the HCF and LCM of two numbers:
HCF x LCM = Product of two Numbers

Example:

Problem: Find the HCF and LCM of 12 and 18, and verify the relationship.

Prime factorisation of 12 is 2 × 2 × 3 = 22 × 3
Prime factorisation of 18 is 2 × 3 × 3 = 2 × 32
HCF(12, 18) = 2 × 3 = 6
LCM(12, 18) = 2 × 2 × 3 × 3 = 36

HCF(12, 18) LCM(12, 18) = 6 × 36 = 216 = 12 × 18.
Hence we verified the relationship :
HCF x LCM = Product of two Numbers
